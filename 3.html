The Perfect Weekend Getaway from New York City

The Lodge at Wood Lodge embraces a philosophy of personal awakening. As so many individuals get swept up in the whirlwind of life, our luxury Poconos resort gives you an opportunity to shift from your everyday routine and re-focus on yourself. You are able to reconnect to creative and stress-relieving outlets that will continue to benefit you long after you leave.


The Lodge Hotels & Resort is an award-winning resort recognized as the #1 Family Resort in the United States by TripAdvisor and the #1 Family Resort by USAToday 10Best. Located just 2 miles from The Lodge at Woodloch, our family resort provides the ideal atmosphere for families and groups alike to re-connect and rebuild lasting relationships. Known for the warm hospitable environment that can only be created by a family-owned and operated business, Woodloch Pines provides guests a multi-generational tradition demonstrated by one of the highest repeat guest ratios in the industry for the past 50+ years proving that spending time with family never goes out of style.

Directions To The Wood Lodge

This spectacular weekend getaway or weekday retreat offers a getaway without the jet-a-way.
